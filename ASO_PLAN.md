# App Store Optimization (ASO) Plan for FocusDown

## 1. App Identity & Positioning
*   **App Name**: FocusDown
*   **Core Value Proposition**: The most intuitive focus timer that enforces discipline by requiring the phone to be face-down.
*   **Target Audience**: Students, Professionals, ADHD users, Digital Detox seekers.

## 2. Competitor Analysis Summary
*   **Forest**: Dominates "Gamified Focus". Key angle: "Plant trees, beat addiction".
*   **Flipd**: Focuses on "Study" and "Social accountability".
*   **Opal**: Focuses on "Screen Time Blocking" and "Deep Focus".
*   **Flat Tomato**: Focuses on "Pomodoro" and "Simplicity".

**Our Winning Angle**: "Physical interaction for digital discipline." The "Flip to Focus" mechanic is our unique hook that bridges the physical and digital worlds.

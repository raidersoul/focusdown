About the App

FocusDown is a minimalist focus timer designed to help you enter a state of flow. Simply flip your phone face down to start the timer and eliminate distractions.

  • Flip-to-Focus: Natural gesture control.
  • Zen Soundscapes: Rain, Forest, Cafe, and more.
  • Stats Hub: Track streaks and focus distribution.
  • Privacy First: Your data stays on your device.
